Graphic Design

Graphic design is a creative discipline that involves combining text, images, and other visual elements to convey messages or information. It encompasses various forms of visual communication, including advertisements, branding, packaging, web design, print media, and more. Graphic designers use their artistic skills and design principles to create visually appealing and effective designs that meet the specific needs and objectives of their clients or projects.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels, platforms, and strategies to promote products, services, or brands. It encompasses various online marketing tactics aimed at reaching and engaging with a target audience, generating leads, driving website traffic, and ultimately, achieving specific business goals.

Icons are often used in various digital interfaces, such as websites, mobile applications, software, and user interfaces (UI), to represent actions, features, or categories.
